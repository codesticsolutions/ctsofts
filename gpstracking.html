How Does Aktovis GPS Work?

Aktovis GPS uses advanced satellite tracking and cellular connectivity to give you real-time updates on your vehicles. Discover exactly where each car is, track speed, monitor driving behavior, set speed alerts and geofences — all through your mobile app or web dashboard.

How To Track A Car

1

Activate GPS Tracker

Scan the QR code provided on the back of your GPS tracker, create your account, and activate the device in just a few minutes.

2

Plug GPS in OBD2 Port

Simply plug the GPS tracker into your car’s OBD2 port. No tools, no wiring, and no professional installation required.

3

Download GPS App

Download the mobile app or access the web dashboard to view real-time location, speed, and route history instantly.

4

Get Answers & Alerts

Receive instant alerts, trip reports, and access customer support whenever you need help or information.
